Fast memory rules

Hard totals: 17+ stand. 13–16 stand against 2–6, hit against 7–A. 12 stands only against 4–6. Double 9 against 3–6, 10 against 2–9, 11 against 2–10.

Soft totals: soft 13–14 double 5–6; soft 15–16 double 4–6; soft 17 double 3–6; soft 18 double 3–6, stand 2/7/8, hit 9/10/A.

Pairs: always split Aces and 8s. Never split 10s or 5s. Split 9s except against 7, 10, Ace. Split 2s/3s/7s against 2–7, 6s against 2–6, 4s only against 5–6.
